Stream music via Spotify Connect: select "Beocreate" as the playback device in the Spotify app. A Spotify Premium subscription is required.

The switch starts or stops the go-librespot service. When it is off, "Beocreate" disappears from the Spotify app. Further settings (device name, audio output) live in ~/.config/go-librespot/config.yml.
